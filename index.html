Business Overview

Why the project exists

The retailer operates several regional store locations and is experiencing frequent inventory errors, inaccurate stock levels, delayed restocking, higher operating costs, lost sales opportunities, and customer dissatisfaction because the current process depends on manual updates and disconnected spreadsheets.

The RIMS implementation supports operational accuracy, customer experience, scalable growth, and data-driven decision-making through real-time visibility and integrated inventory data.

Project Purpose & Objectives

Target outcomes

01Improve inventory accuracy by reducing human error and automating stock updates.

05Deliver a scalable system for future growth and expansion.

Project Scope

Included vs. excluded

Included in Scope

  • Inventory management software and handheld scanners
  • Integration with the existing POS system
  • Current inventory data migration
  • Staff training and change management
  • Data backup and recovery procedures

Excluded from Scope

  • POS system redevelopment
  • Expansion to locations outside the current network infrastructure
  • Marketing or customer-facing system enhancements
